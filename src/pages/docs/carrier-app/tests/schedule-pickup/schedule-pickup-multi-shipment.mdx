Carrier App Tests
==================================

### `schedulePickup_multi_shipment`

This test exercises your app's ability to successfully schedule a pickup with multiple shipments. If your app does not have any [pickup services](../../../reference/pickup-service.mdx), then this test will be skipped by [the `connect test` command](../../../cli.mdx#test-your-app).
