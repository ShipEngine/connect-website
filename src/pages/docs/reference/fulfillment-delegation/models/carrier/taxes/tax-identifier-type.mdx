TaxIdentifierType
===============================================
An Enum of the possible values for supported tax identification types


<Reference>  
  <Field name="TIN">
    <Description>
      "tin" - Tax Identification Number
    </Description>
  </Field>
  <Field name="EIN">
    <Description>
      "ein" - Employer Identification Number
    </Description>
  </Field>
  <Field name="SSN">
    <Description>
      "ssn" - Social Security Number
    </Description>
  </Field>
  <Field name="VAT">
    <Description>
      "vat" - Value Added Tax Identification Number
    </Description>
  </Field>
  <Field name="EORI">
    <Description>
      "eori" - Economic Operators Registration and Identification Number
    </Description>
  </Field>
  <Field name="IOSS">
    <Description>
      "ioss" - The IOSS allows suppliers and electronic interfaces selling imported goods to buyers in the EU to collect, declare and pay the VAT to the tax authorities, instead of making the buyer pay the VAT at the moment the goods are imported into the EU as it was previously the case (for products over 22 EUR) (https://ec.europa.eu/taxation_customs/business/vat/ioss_en)
    </Description>
  </Field>
  <Field name="PAN">
    <Description>
      "pan" - A ten-character alphanumeric identifier, issued by the Indian Income Tax
    </Description>
  </Field>
  <Field name="VOEC">
    <Description>
      "voec" - Norway's VAT on E-Commerce number (https://www.skatteetaten.no/en/business-and-organisation/vat-and-duties/vat/foreign/e-commerce-voec/)
    </Description>
  </Field>
</Reference>
