Order App Tests
==================================

### `getSalesOrdersByDate`

This test exercises your app's ability to successfully retrieve sales orders by a date range.
If your app's `getSalesOrdersByDate` method 
implements [paging](../../../reference/methods/get-sales-orders-by-date) then this test will continuosly call the `getSalesOrdersByDate` until the 
`paging.cursor` property is returned as `undefined`.
